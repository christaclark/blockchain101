1. How it Works

In it’s simplest form a blockchain is a way to track payments or transactions without the need for a central, 3rd party such as a bank or VISA to process the transaction. Every transaction is copied across every node in the network. This technology is revolutionary for several reasons. The way is how you can use it to store data.. Without a database. I know, it’s crazy, but bare with me. If you aren’t familiar with databases hang in there, if you are: you write once then it becomes a read only.
Block - List of latest transactions that were just processed.
Chain - List is attached to previous blocks and becomes permanent (meaning you can’t edit it).
